Finding orders

All your previous orders are stored in our archives. You can access them by clicking one of the links under the MY ORDERS category in the left menu.

Watch a Flash demonstration on this topic

Find a specific order

You can find a speciffic order using the Find option under the MY ORDERS category.

The system can locate your order if you enter text used in that order or the reference number.

My reference

The My reference field at the bottom of the order form makes order searching easy for you in the future. This field’s purpose is only for you reference and you can type in any text-keyword or number that you think will help you find that particular order when needed.
