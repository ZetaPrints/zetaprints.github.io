New user registration

Anyone with email address @westpac.co.nz can register on the website and place an order.

Click on Register link at the top-right corner of the page

Provide your email address

Fill in a form with your details

Check your inbox for a confirmation email few minutes later

  • if the confirmation email does not arrive check your junk mail folder
  • if the confirmation email is nowhere to be found ask your supervisor
  • if your supervisor doesn’t hold your emails then go back to the site, check the email address and ask for another copy to be emailed to you
  • contact eBuy technical support if nothing else works

When the email arrives click on the link to confirm your identity

  • if the link looks strange and broken you may need to piece it together because your email client split it in 2 lines
  • forward the email to eBuy technical support if you need help with piecing together the confirmation link

Once registered you can place orders for stationery products listed on the site.
