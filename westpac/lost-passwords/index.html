Lost passwords

Some existing eBuy users have been pre-loaded into the database with passwords set to default as indicated in the email circulated to staff prior to launch. Follow the instructions here if the default password doesn’t work for you or watch a demonstration

.

  1. Click on Lost Password under the login form.
  2. Enter your email address and the numbers/letters from the image shown just under the form. They are required to prove you are a human avoid robots trying to hack into your account.
  3. Click on Email me my password.
  4. Check your email in a few minutes. If there is still no password, check your Junk Mail folder.
  5. Log in using your password.

You can change it after logging in by clicking on My Details at the top-left corner of the screen.

Unregistered users

If your email address is not registered in our database, please, follow the instructions on the screen to register. You will get access immediately after confirming your email address. Registration takes less than a minute.

Getting assistance

Use the feedback form on the page or email support@crucialcolour.com if you need help. We’ll be glad to assist you.
