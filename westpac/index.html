General Instructions

To access the new Domiciled Stationery Ordering site
Click on the Domiciled Stationery link from within eBuy.
…..and follow the below instructions.

Step One

Enter your email address and password.

For the launch of this new system your password has been set to your eBuy ID.

NOTE:
Passwords are case sensitive.

  • You can alter this password within the site by clicking on “My Details”. (PIC 2 Arrow)
  • If you can not get your password to work, then click on “I lost my password” and your password will be emailed to you instantly.

Step Two

Find the card that is relevant to you by using the scroll bar on the right hand side of the screen
(PIC 3).

Click on ‘Place an order’
(PIC 3 – Arrow 1)

Step Three

Complete all the relevant fields.

Your business card will fit up to 5 telephone fields comfortably, but there are 8 different options.

In the last field there is an option for a Customer Service or Merchant Number (PIC 5).

In the last field there is an option for a Customer Service or Merchant Number (PIC 5). If this number is selected then the details in PIC’s 6 & 7 will appear on your card.

You will only be able to fit on your card up to 3 contact fax or phone numbers.

Step Four

Click ‘Update Preview’ when you have completed all the fields.

A card preview will generate.

To alter the card further, simply check the fields & make changes and click ‘Update Preview’ again until you are happy

TIP
If you do not want this detail to appear on your card then select the blank part of the drop down box. (See PIC 9).

Step Five

  1. Enter the quantity you require.
  2. Type in your cost centre.
  3. Type in a delivery address. If it is different than what is on the card.
  4. Click on ‘Order’.

Step Six

You will automatically be taken to the top of the screen.

Click ‘Checkout’.

PIC 10

Order Confirmation

You will shortly receive an email confirmation back to you including a picture of what you have ordered, the quantity and delivery address.

Tracking your order

The confirmation email will also include a link back to the site when if clicked on will take you to see the status of your order. It will either say “To Print”, “In Print” or “Shipped”.
