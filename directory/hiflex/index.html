HIFLEX

Web-to-print software
09/03/2011

http://www.hiflex.com/

HIFLEX is a German company that deals with Business Automation Systems, mainly MIS and JDF software. HIFLEX Webshop, their Web to Print solution, is a new SaaS offering developed through Drupal. It has basic customer and corporate storefront and shopping cart   features. Storefront branding, interactive customization of print products, dynamic imaging and variable data printing are NOT available. Extending the functionality is possible through their API, potentially.
