Cendix

Web-to-print software
18/11/2011

http://www.cendix.com/

Cendix emphasis is on direct marketing, digital and postal mail.

From the looks of it, their web-to-print involvement starts and ends with direct mail letters and postcards generation. The company seems concentrated on providing on-demand application development tailored to the requirements of specific clients as well as marketing and technology consulting.

The only identifiable customer of Cendix is :

  • ZairMail

The following websites used to use them, but it now looks like they moved on:

  • Instaletter
  • GetGordon
  • Synotac
  • DevonWay

Discontinued

Cendix website has copyright date range up to 2007, which is probably when they stopped working on the project. The only live example of their technology looks abandoned too.

Web-to-print powered by Cendix

ZairMail web-to-print ordering process is powered by Cendix. It is rather basic and is illustrated in the screenshots below.

There was no opportunity to do any online editing and the uploaded photo got distorted (stretched) to fill the page.
