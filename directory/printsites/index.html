PrintSites

Web-to-print software
28/03/2011

http://printsites.com/

PrintSites has been providing print management technology since 2009.  One of the main features of their web-to print system is the PrintSites Design Studio which allows interactive customization of designs and templates.  Through a web storefront, customers may upload their own design or change the background, text and images on a pdf template of a print product.

Other features of their web to print solutions package include digital proofing and approval utilities, and marketing tools such as broadcast email, discount and promo code utilities are also available.   For their websites, clients have 2 options: they may build an entirely new e-commerce website, or they may link their old site to one that will be hosted by PrintSites.

It is not specified if PrintSites’s technology can be integrated with other systems and if it has variable data printing capabilities.
